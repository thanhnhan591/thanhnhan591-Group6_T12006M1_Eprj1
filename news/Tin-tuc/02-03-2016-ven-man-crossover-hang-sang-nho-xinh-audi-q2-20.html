02/03/2016 Revealing the small luxury crossover Audi Q2

The all-new small luxury crossover Audi Q2 has been launched on the eve of the Geneva show. It seems that Audi has taken quite a while to develop Q2. The car is expected to be available on the European market this fall. However, Audi will open a customer order receipt book from mid-2016.

Details


Audi Q2 has a total of 6 different engine types in the European market, including 3 gasoline engines and 3 diesel engines. The first is a gasoline engine TFSI, 3 cylinders, capacity 1.0 liter with a maximum capacity of 116 horsepower. The second is a petrol engine TFSI, 1.4 liter capacity with a maximum capacity of 150 horsepower. The corresponding figure of the 4-cylinder TFSI petrol engine, with a capacity of 2.0 liters with the technology of stopping the cylinder is 190 horsepower.

In addition, Audi also offers Drive Select system with 4 driving modes: Auto (automatic), Comfort (comfortable), Dynamic (flexible) and Efficiency (economical) for the new Q2. With the version with the MMI radio, the Audi Q2 will have an Individual driving mode (individual). Finally, there is an optional ESC Offroad driving mode with an increased ground clearance of 200 mm.
 
True to the tradition of the Audi family, the Q2 also comes with a lot of modern technology. The Audi Q2 virtual cockpit with the information display on the windshield and the 12.3-inch screen. Besides the adaptive cruise control system, the Stop & function is also available. Go, traffic jam assist, side assistance, lane keeping assist, traffic sign recognition, and parking assist.
 
The standard Audi Q2 infotainment system can be controlled via push button and swivel. The MMI navigation feature with touchpad on the center console is optional equipment. Finally, there is a Wifi system for connecting smartphones.
 
When available on the market, the Audi Q2 comes with four standard equipment, Design Line, Sport Line and S Line. Particularly, the Audi Q2 S Line has yellow, orange and red accents as a distinctive feature. Depending on the model, the Audi Q2 will come with white or aluminum plastic trim accessories. Finally, there are Nappa leather seats and LED lights.
