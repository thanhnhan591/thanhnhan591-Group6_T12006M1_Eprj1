Warranty program

  •  
  • Tweet
1. We only accept orders when providing enough accurate information about the address, phone number. After you place an order, we will contact you again to check information and agree on more relevant things.

2. Some sensitive cases: the order value is too large & the delivery time at night, the delivery address is in the lane or may lead to danger. We will actively contact you to agree on specific delivery time.

3. In case of delayed delivery without prior notice, you may not receive the item and we will refund the full amount you prepaid (if any) within 7 days.

Step 1: Access the website and select the products to buy to buy

Step 2: Click on the product you want to buy, and the screen will pop up with the following options

If you want to continue buying: Click on continue to purchase to choose to add products to the cart

If you want to view cart to update products: Click view cart

If you want to order and pay for this product please click: Place an order and pay

Step 3: Select payment account information

If you already have an account, please enter your username as email and password into the section that already has an account on the system.

If you do not have an account and want to register an account please fill in personal information to continue to register for an account. When you have an account you will easily track your orders

If you want to purchase without an account please click on the order without an account

Step 4: Fill in your information to receive an order, choose a form of payment and shipping for your order

Step 5: Review order information, fill in comments and submit orders

After receiving your order, we will contact you by calling again to confirm your order and address.

Sincerely thank you.
